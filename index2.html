I am a PhD student at MIT, advised by Bill Freeman and Josh Tenenbaum. I received my undergraduate degree from Tsinghua University, working with Zhuowen Tu. I have also spent time at research labs of Microsoft, Facebook, and Baidu. My research has been supported by fellowships from Facebook, Nvidia, Samsung, Baidu, and Adobe.

I study machine perception, reasoning, and its interaction with the physical world, drawing inspiration from human cognition.
